About Me

My name is Brittani Hope Drost — a writer, artist, and graphic designer based in Berkeley, California. Deep and effective communication is a passion of mine and one I strive to achieve in all manner of artistic pursuits, such as through the written word, ink drawings, even embroidery! -- and, of course, through graphic design work.

I am an empathetic and incredibly dedicated designer who will work hard to bring your ideas to the world in colorful and creative ways. Would you like something drawn? Something written? Do you need a fresh and spiffy brochure design for your next trade-show? Whatever your needs, I have the versatility to exceed your expectations. Please see below for some of the skills and services I can provide.

I am seeking a team of other skilled professionals to work and learn with side by side.  If you are in need of collaborations, commissions, or other inquiries, I would love to hear from you!
